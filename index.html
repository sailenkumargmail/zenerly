Common questions

How is per-employee pricing calculated?
Pricing is based on active employees — anyone on payroll or tracked within Zenerly in a given month. Deactivated or offboarded employees are never counted in your bill.
Can I manage multiple countries from one account?
Yes. Starter supports one payroll jurisdiction. Professional supports up to 5 countries. Enterprise supports unlimited countries with full local law and tax compliance auto-configured.
Is CareStream included in HRMS plans?
CareStream is available as an add-on to any HRMS plan or as a standalone product. Bundled pricing is typically 20–30% lower — contact our team for a custom quote.
Is there a free trial?
All plans include a 30-day free trial with full feature access and up to 25 employees. No credit card required — export your data at any time with zero friction.
What currencies do you invoice in?
We invoice in SGD, USD, GBP, AUD, INR, MYR, AED, IDR, PHP, and THB. Prices shown on this page reflect your local currency at the rates locked in at signup.
