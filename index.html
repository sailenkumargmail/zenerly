Common questions

How is pricing calculated?
Pricing is per active employee per month, billed annually. An "active employee" is any person on your payroll or tracked within Zenerly in a given month. You're never charged for deactivated or offboarded employees.
Can I run multiple countries on a single account?
Yes. Starter supports a single payroll jurisdiction. Professional supports up to 5 countries from one admin account. Enterprise has no limit — we support 18 countries with full localisation.
Is there a free trial?
All plans include a 30-day free trial with full feature access and up to 25 employees. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you choose your plan or export your data with zero friction.
What currencies do you accept?
We accept payment in SGD, USD, GBP, AUD, INR, MYR, AED, and IDR. Invoices are always issued in your local currency at the rates shown on this page.
Can I add Fraud Detection or Compliance as add-ons?
Fraud Detection and Compliance modules are available as standalone add-ons to any HRMS plan. Contact our sales team for bundled pricing that's typically 20–30% lower than buying separately.
